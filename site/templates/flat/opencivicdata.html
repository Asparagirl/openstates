The Sunlight Foundation is seeking volunteers to assist in developing a national collection of civic data about our cities and towns. This municipal-level legislative data will echo that available from our Open States Project for all 50 states. Whether you can write a web scraper, help with data management, or contact policy makers on why open data is important, you contributions are valued.

We are committed to creating a free, open and sustainable database of civic data, covering municipalities across the U.S. Additionally, we are encouraging all local governments, technology vendors and web developers to adopt a set of Open Civic Data standards to ensure that data is made available publicly and in a machine-readable format.

This collection of local legislative data is also part of an API, currently in beta. Sunlight also welcomes our community partners in this project and supporters such as Google.org. Read reading for information on how to get involved.
